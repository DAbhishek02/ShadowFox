Indian Premier League (IPL)

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is the world's most popular and glamorous professional Twenty20 cricket league, held annually in India. Since its inception by the BCCI in 2008, the IPL has redefined cricket with its blend of sports, entertainment, and global talent.

Key Features

  • Founded: 2008
  • Format: Fast-paced Twenty20 (T20) matches
  • Teams: 10 city-based franchises
  • Season: March to May every year
  • Audience: Watched by millions worldwide, both in stadiums and on TV/streaming
  • Known for: Star players, thrilling finishes, cheerleaders, and grand opening ceremonies

Why IPL is Special

  • Brings together the best international and Indian cricket talent
  • Massive fan engagement, both in stadiums and online
  • Innovative formats like player auctions and strategic timeouts
  • Platform for young players to shine on a global stage
  • Significant economic and cultural impact in India

Fun Facts

  • MS Dhoni and Rohit Sharma are among the most successful IPL captains
  • IPL is broadcast in over 120 countries
  • Known for record-breaking player auctions and high-octane matches
